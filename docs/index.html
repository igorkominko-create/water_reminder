1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy describes how [Developer Name] (“we”, “us”) collects, uses, and shares information when you use the mobile application [App Name] (the “App”) on iOS and Android.

By using the App, you agree to this Privacy Policy. If you do not agree, please do not use the App.

2. Information we collect

Depending on how you use the App, we may process the following categories of data:

  • Hydration and app settings — daily water intake you log, daily goal, and related preferences. This data is stored locally on your device (and in the App Group shared container used for home screen widgets on iOS).
  • Device and app diagnostics — basic technical data needed to keep the App stable (for example, crash information if provided by the operating system).
  • Cross-app links — if you tap a SnapBite promotion in the App, we open the SnapBite listing in the App Store or Google Play. We do not receive your hydration data when you do this.

We do not require you to create an account to use the core features of the App, and we do not knowingly collect payment information through the App.

3. How we use information

  • To provide hydration tracking, goals, reminders, and home screen widgets.
  • To sync widget display with data you enter in the App.
  • To show optional SnapBite cross-promotion (our AI nutrition app) when you choose to open it.
  • To comply with legal obligations and protect our rights.

4. SnapBite cross-promotion

The App may show a banner or dialog promoting SnapBite, our separate AI calorie scanner app. Tapping it opens the store listing in your device’s app marketplace (App Store or Google Play). We do not embed third-party advertising SDKs in this App.

5. Data storage and retention

Hydration data and settings are stored primarily on your device. We do not operate a user account server for the App at this time.

Data remains on your device until you delete the App, reset today’s intake in the App, or clear app data through your device settings. Widget data on iOS is stored in the App Group container shared between the App and its widget extension.

6. Sharing of information

We may share information only in these situations:

  • App stores — when you open a SnapBite store link, Apple or Google handle that request under their own policies.
  • Legal requirements — if required by law, court order, or to protect safety and rights.
  • Business transfers — in connection with a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets, with notice where required by law.

We do not sell your personal information.

7. Children’s privacy

The App is not directed to children under 13 (or the minimum age required in your country). We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided us information, contact us and we will take appropriate steps to delete it.

8. Your rights and choices

Depending on your location, you may have rights to access, correct, or delete personal data we hold about you.

  • You can change your daily goal and reset intake in the App settings.
  • You can uninstall the App to remove locally stored data.
  • You can ignore or dismiss SnapBite promotions without sharing hydration data.

If you are in the EEA, UK, or similar regions, you may also have rights under GDPR / UK GDPR. Contact us using the details below to exercise your rights.
